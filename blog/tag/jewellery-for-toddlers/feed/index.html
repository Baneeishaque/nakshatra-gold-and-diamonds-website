Nowadays every brand has a made a priority for themselves to make a range of jewellery for toddlers, unlike the archaic days of toddlers sporting adult sized jewellery. Our range of jewellery is categorically designed with toddlers in mind. Here are some of our offerings for toddlers.

Earrings – Conservatively designed with a single stone stud setup for ease of use and lightweight.
Bangles and bracelets – One of the most popular choices of gold pieces for toddlers, our collection of bangles and bracelets are designed with a lightweight approach to fit in the delicate hands of our toddlers.

Anklets – Another popular form of gold when it comes to toddlers, as they can be adorned by both boys and girls they make an excellent choice for gifts in the case of impending arrival of a bundle of joy. This is also a popular choice for many due to safety reasons.

Chains – Not the most practical piece of jewellery when it comes to toddlers for reasons of safety, most parents buy chains as an accessory piece for a special occasion rather than an everyday jewellery.
